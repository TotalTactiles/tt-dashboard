# Project Overview

## What This System Is
A real-time business intelligence dashboard for Total Tactiles (TT Business) — a tactile paving and accessibility products company. The system aggregates CRM deal data, revenue, costs, cashflow, and operational metrics into a single live dashboard used by sales, finance, and operations leadership.

## Problem It Solves
- Deal pipeline data lives in Zoho CRM but cannot be visualised or sliced without custom reports
- Revenue and COGS tracking are manual spreadsheet exercises with no live view
- Cashflow forecasting is disconnected from actual invoiced revenue
- No single screen shows quote-to-cash conversion, gross margins, and upcoming cashflow
- Goal tracking against financial targets requires manual calculation

## Who Uses It
- Business owner / director (daily operational decisions)
- Sales team (pipeline view, deal status)
- Finance (revenue, cashflow)
- System actors: n8n, Zoho CRM, Google Sheets

## End Goal
A zero-touch live dashboard where Zoho CRM is the single data entry point. All downstream metrics — pipeline value, revenue, COGS, cashflow position, and goal tracking — update automatically within minutes of a deal stage change. No manual spreadsheet entry required.
